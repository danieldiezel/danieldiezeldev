Biography

Authenticity is my greatest strength.

Daniel Diezel, born and raised in Aschaffenburg, embodies a lifestyle built on passion, discipline and authenticity. As an actor and content creator, he has built a community of over 400,000 people – people who value his unfiltered perspective on sport, lifestyle and career.

Alongside more than 20 years of football – including over a decade at TuS Aschaffenburg Leider – Daniel has found his way in front of the camera: TV productions like ARD's "Verstehen Sie Spaß?" and commercial shoots showcase a versatile talent.

Whether on set, in the content world or out on the pitch – Daniel brings the same energy: grounded, ambitious and always himself.

Career

The road to here.

2004
First steps at SV Stockstadt
The beginning of a long football career. Daniel's instinct in front of goal becomes apparent early – already in youth football he scores prolifically.
2008
Historic youth championship
With a goal difference of 48:0, Stockstadt clinches the title – a season that is still legendary in the region to this day.
2013
Move to TuS Aschaffenburg Leider
After 114 matches and over 155 goals for Stockstadt, the next step follows: Daniel joins Aschaffenburg's powerhouse TuS Leider.
2019
City Champion with TuS Aschaffenburg Leider
The highlight of his club career: Daniel wins the city championship with TuS Leider – a title the whole team had been working towards.
2024
Building the social media community
Daniel builds a community of over 400,000 followers across YouTube, Instagram and TikTok – with content covering sport, lifestyle and everyday life.
2025
TV debut: ARD "Verstehen Sie Spaß?"
Daniel makes his TV debut in one of Germany's most beloved entertainment shows – in a lead role as a balloon supplier and a supporting role as a construction worker.
2025
Commercial for Tilly Hedrich GmbH
Alongside his TV appearance, Daniel shoots his first commercial – as lead actor in a humorous spot for lubricant manufacturer Tilly Hedrich GmbH.
