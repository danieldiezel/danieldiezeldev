Series Appearances

Construction worker- Side Character

In another episode of Verstehen Sie Spaß?, Daniel took on a striking supporting role as a construction worker. Together with his colleagues, he played a key part in both the execution and the eventual reveal of the prank. Notably, he was responsible for the seemingly “disappearing” vehicle, which he subtly removed as part of the setup. With his natural presence and keen sense of timing, Daniel contributed significantly to making the scene both authentic and entertaining.
