Side Character

Construction Worker

In another episode of Verstehen Sie Spaß?, Daniel took on a sharp supporting role as a construction worker. Together with his colleagues, he was centrally involved in both carrying out the prank and revealing it afterward. Particularly notable was his role in the apparent "disappearance" of a vehicle, which he covertly removed as part of the setup. With natural presence and a fine sense of timing, Daniel helped make the situation feel authentic and entertaining.
