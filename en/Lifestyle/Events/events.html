Welcome to my Lifestyle - Events

Events

As an influencer, I have had the opportunity to attend various exciting events. Here are some of the notable events I have been a part of:

  • Beerpong Turnier Steckster Gadde

    Daniel and friends came up with the idea of a beer pong tournament between friends. This idea developed very quickly and took on great proportions when the tournament was accompanied by Daniel's social media channel. Almost 1 million people watched the videos of the tournament on the internet. "The first self-organized tournament and then such a success, simply a dream" - Daniel, on the successful tournament and the social media success of the tournament
  • Oktoberfest 2023

    Daniel had the incredible opportunity to attend Oktoberfest 2023 in Munich, and it was an unforgettable experience. Despite the large crowds, he and his eight friends enjoyed the festivities from a gallery table, which provided them with some much-needed distance from the bustling masses. The lively atmosphere, filled with music, laughter, and celebration, made the evening truly special. Having their own space allowed them to fully enjoy the vibrant spirit of Oktoberfest without feeling overwhelmed, making it a memorable event for Daniel and his friends.
  • Videodays 2023

    Daniel attended VideoDays, Germany's largest creator festival, where the motto "by creators for creators" truly came to life. The event provided a unique platform for the creator and media industry, bringing together Germany's biggest and most influential creators in one place. Daniel participated in networking sessions, exchanged ideas, and joined masterclasses that offered valuable tips and tricks from industry leaders. Reflecting on the experience, he said, "It was wonderful to reconnect with friends and colleagues. There's always something special about meeting new people in such a harmonious environment." The festival also featured an Experience Area, where Daniel engaged with well-known lovebrands, making the day both productive and enjoyable.
  • NFL-Frankfurt Games 2023

    Daniel had the incredible honor of being invited as a special guest to the NFL Frankfurt Games 2023, where he attended the pregame party and the thrilling Chiefs vs. Dolphins game. Seated with NFL officials at the party, Daniel enjoyed an unforgettable evening filled with great conversations, laughter, and a true sense of camaraderie. Reflecting on the experience, Daniel shared how much fun he had, surrounded by passionate football fans and industry insiders, making it a memorable and exciting event.
  • Fanfest 2024 EM Germany

    Daniel had the privilege of being invited as a special guest to the EM Fanfest 2024 in Germany, where he also took part in the public viewing events. He thoroughly enjoyed the vibrant atmosphere, surrounded by enthusiastic fans celebrating together. Reflecting on the experience, Daniel said, "Public viewings during the EM are just beautiful, shared parties." The event was filled with excitement and unity, making it a truly memorable occasion for Daniel.
  • World Club Dome 2024

    Daniel had the incredible opportunity to attend the World Club Dome (WCD) as a special guest, where he immersed himself in the electrifying atmosphere of one of the biggest music festivals in the world. Surrounded by a sea of passionate fans and pulsating beats, Daniel enjoyed every moment of the event. Reflecting on his experience, Daniel shared, "The energy at WCD is unmatched – it's a massive celebration of music and unity." The festival was an unforgettable highlight for him, filled with epic performances, great vibes, and a shared love for electronic music.
  • Videodays 2024

    Daniel Diezel was part of this year’s VideoDays Festival 2024, one of the largest gatherings for content creators and fans. In an interview, he shared his excitement: “It’s great to see old friends again, but also to make so many new ones. The people here are simply wonderful.” The festival provided the perfect opportunity for exciting connections, inspiring exchanges, and unforgettable experiences within the creator community.
