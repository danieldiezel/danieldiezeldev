Events & Highlights

As an influencer I've had the opportunity to attend some incredible events. Here are some of the most memorable highlights:

Beer Pong Tournament Steckster Gadde

Daniel and friends organised a beer pong tournament that quickly went viral through Daniel's social media channel – almost 1 million views. "The first self-organised tournament and such a success, simply a dream." – Daniel

Oktoberfest 2023

Daniel attended Oktoberfest 2023 in Munich with eight friends, enjoying the festivities from a gallery table. The lively atmosphere filled with music, laughter and celebration made for an unforgettable evening.

Videodays 2023

Daniel attended Germany's largest creator festival, joining networking sessions and masterclasses. "It was wonderful to reconnect with friends and colleagues – there's always something special about it." – Daniel

NFL Frankfurt Games 2023

Invited as a special guest to the NFL Frankfurt Games 2023, Daniel attended the pre-game party and the thrilling Chiefs vs. Dolphins game alongside NFL officials – a memorable evening of great conversations and camaraderie.

Fanfest 2024 EM Germany

As a special guest at the EM Fanfest 2024, Daniel joined public viewing events surrounded by enthusiastic fans. "Public viewings during the EM are just beautiful, shared parties." – Daniel

World Club Dome 2024

As a special guest at the World Club Dome, Daniel immersed himself in the electrifying atmosphere of one of the world's biggest music festivals. "The energy at WCD is unmatched – a massive celebration of music and unity." – Daniel

Videodays 2024

Daniel was part of the VideoDays Festival 2024, one of the largest gatherings for content creators and fans. "It's great to see old friends again, but also to make so many new ones. The people here are simply wonderful." – Daniel
