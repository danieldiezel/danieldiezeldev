Den Haag

Accommodation

The Penthouse Appartments

For your accommodation in The Hague, I highly recommend The Hague Tower, The Penthouse. Situated in the tallest tower, this stunning property offers unparalleled panoramic views of the city. Prepare to be amazed as you gaze out over the beautiful skyline from the comfort of your living room. What sets this accommodation apart is the exclusive private cinema, where you can enjoy a cinematic experience like no other. Immerse yourself in luxury, convenience, and breathtaking sights at The Hague Tower, The Penthouse.

Restaurant Tip

De Pier Pannenkoeken and Grill

This restaurant offers a super beautiful view of the sea and sunset. The fish served here is exceptionally delicious. The portions may be slightly smaller than expected, but considering the price, it is still worth it. Enjoy a memorable dining experience with a breathtaking view at De Pier Pannenkoeken.

Attraction

Mauritstoren

Visit the magnificent Mauritstoren in Den Haag. This huge and stunning building has a must-see courtyard. A guided tour inside is highly recommended as a secret tip. Don't miss out on this worthwhile experience.

Hidden Gem

China Town

For a unique and vibrant experience, make sure to visit China Town. This hidden gem is packed with local Chinese shops offering a wide variety of foods. Indulge in the delicious cuisine and soak up the lively atmosphere. Dining here is a must-try to truly catch the vibe of China Town.
