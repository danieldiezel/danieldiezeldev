Den Haag

Accommodation

The Penthouse Appartments

For your accommodation in The Hague, I highly recommend The Hague Tower, The Penthouse. Situated in the tallest tower, this stunning property offers unparalleled panoramic views of the city. What sets it apart is the exclusive private cinema – an experience like no other, wrapped in luxury and breathtaking sights.

Restaurant Tip

De Pier Pannenkoeken and Grill

This restaurant offers a beautiful view of the sea and sunset. The fish served here is exceptionally delicious. The portions may be slightly smaller than expected, but considering the price it is absolutely worth every cent.

Attraction

Mauritstoren

Visit the magnificent Mauritstoren in Den Haag. This huge and stunning building has a must-see courtyard. A guided tour inside is highly recommended as a hidden tip – don't miss out on this worthwhile experience.

Hidden Gem

China Town

For a unique and vibrant experience, make sure to visit China Town. This hidden gem is packed with local Chinese shops offering a wide variety of foods. Dining here is a must to truly catch the vibe of the neighbourhood.
