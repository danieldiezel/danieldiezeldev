Bavarian Forest

Accommodation

BERGlässig Hotel Bodenmais

The BERGlässig Hotel in Bodenmais is the ideal accommodation – its unique charm and cozy atmosphere offer a truly relaxing and unforgettable stay. The picturesque location in the Bavarian Forest, excellent service and comfortable amenities make it the perfect retreat.

Restaurant Tip

Eisensteiner Hütte

An unforgettable experience at the Eisensteiner Hütte – enchanting ambiance, a breathtaking view over the valley and the summit of the Arber, regional specialties prepared to perfection. An experience I would gladly repeat.

Attraction

Silberbergbad

The Silberbergbad in Bodenmais offers both a relaxing atmosphere and a stunning view of the Silberberg. The wide range of wellness options and the spacious sauna area provide the perfect opportunity to unwind and escape everyday stress.

Hidden Gem

Skiing at the Arber

A fantastic time skiing at the Großer Arber – diverse slopes for all skill levels, a toboggan run for the whole family and an exciting timed race course. Highly recommended for families and adventure seekers alike.
