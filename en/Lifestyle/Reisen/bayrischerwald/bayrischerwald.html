Bayrischer Wald

Accommodation

BERGlässig Hotel Bodenmais From my personal perspective, the BERGlässig Hotel in Bodenmais is the ideal accommodation, as it offers a truly relaxing and unforgettable stay with its unique charm and cozy atmosphere. Its picturesque location in the Bavarian Forest, combined with excellent service and comfortable amenities, makes it the perfect retreat for anyone seeking both tranquility and adventure.

Restaurant Tipp

Eisensteiner Hütte I had an unforgettable experience at the Eisensteiner Hütte. The ambiance was simply enchanting, with a breathtaking view over the valley and the summit of the Arber. The cozy atmosphere and rustic design of the hut contributed to a unique experience. The food was equally delicious, featuring regional specialties prepared to perfection. Overall, it was a truly impressive and highly recommended experience that I would gladly repeat.

Attraction

Silberbergbad The Silberbergbad in Bodenmais is an impressive destination, offering both a relaxing atmosphere and a stunning view of the Silberberg. The wide range of wellness options and the spacious sauna area provide a wonderful opportunity to unwind and escape everyday stress. Especially noteworthy is the view of the Silberberg, which makes the experience even more special. The sauna and wellness areas are perfectly designed to offer guests a moment of peace and recovery – an unforgettable experience for body and soul.

Hidden Gem

Skiing at the Arber We had a fantastic time skiing at the Großer Arber, which impresses with a variety of diverse slopes and trails. Particularly noteworthy are the challenging runs for all skill levels, the toboggan run for the whole family, and the exciting time trial course for those wanting to test their speed. There’s also a parkour trail for those looking for fun beyond the traditional slopes. An unforgettable experience for skiers and winter sports enthusiasts of all kinds – highly recommended, especially for families and adventure seekers.
