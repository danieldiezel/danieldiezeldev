Lifestyle · Travel

Berlin

Accommodation

Hotel Astoria Waldorf

From my personal perspective, the Astoria Waldorf in Berlin is the ideal accommodation – its impeccable luxury and convenient location deliver a truly indulgent and memorable stay.

Restaurant Tip

Bun Sushi Bar

I had an amazing experience at Bun Sushi Bar. The food was incredibly tasty, with a range of flavors in each bite. The beautiful interior design creates a pleasant atmosphere – overall a memorable and highly recommended dining experience.

Attraction

Brandenburg Gate

The Brandenburg Gate is a historic landmark that is truly captivating. The adjacent Bundestag adds to the area's political significance. Visiting these landmarks provides a memorable experience showcasing Berlin's rich history.

Hidden Gem

Bricks

We had an amazing time at Bricks, with a lively atmosphere and an energetic dance floor. Highly recommended, but consider making a reservation for the VIP section to get the full experience.
