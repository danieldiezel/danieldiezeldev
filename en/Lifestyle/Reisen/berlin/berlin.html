Berlin

Accommodation

Hotel Astoria Waldorf From my personal perspective, the Astoria Waldorf in Berlin is the ideal accommodation due to its impeccable luxury and convenient location, providing a truly indulgent and memorable stay.

Restaurant Tip

Bun Sushi Bar I had an amazing experience at Bun Sushi Bar. The food was incredibly tasty, with a range of flavors in each bite. The interior design was beautiful, creating a pleasant atmosphere. Overall, it was a memorable and highly recommended dining experience.

Attraction

Brandenburger Tor The Brandenburg Gate is a historic landmark that is truly captivating. The adjacent Bundestag adds to the area's political significance. Visiting these landmarks provides a memorable experience, showcasing Berlin's rich history and vibrant political atmosphere.

Hidden Gem

Bricks We had an amazing time at Bricks, with a lively atmosphere and an energetic dance floor. Although the VIP area was crowded, we found our own space near the dance floor and enjoyed the fantastic music and drinks. Highly recommended, but consider making a reservation for the VIP section.
